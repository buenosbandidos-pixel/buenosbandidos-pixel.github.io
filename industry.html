Industry

Buenos Bandidos Producción is an international film production company based in Barcelona. We develop, package and produce feature films and series in collaboration with European and international partners.

What we do

We develop, package and produce feature films and series with strong authorship and international circulation potential. We work across borders and build collaborations early — from development through financing and production to sales conversations.

We’re open to

  • Co-productions and co-development
  • Packaging and financing discussions
  • Conversations with sales agents and distributors

Founders

Juha Kukkonen is a producer / writer / actor and former Artistic Director & CEO of Ryhmäteatteri (2010–2026), one of Finland’s leading theatres. He develops international feature and TV projects between Finland and Spain.

Jarmo Lampela is a producer / writer / director with extensive experience in feature films and television. He develops director-driven projects across development, packaging and production.

The founders have a long-standing collaboration through the Finnish production collective Vegetarian Films (creative and credit history — not a legal affiliation).
