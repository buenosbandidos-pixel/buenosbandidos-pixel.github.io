What we do

We develop, package and produce feature films and series with strong authorship and international circulation potential. We work across borders and build collaborations early — from development through financing and production to sales conversations.

We're open to

  • Co-productions and co-development
  • Packaging and financing discussions
  • Conversations with sales agents and distributors

Founders

Juha Kukkonen is a producer, writer and actor — former Artistic Director & CEO of Ryhmäteatteri (2010–2026), one of Finland's leading theatres. He develops international feature and TV projects between Finland and Spain.

Jarmo Lampela is a producer, writer and director with extensive experience in feature films and television. He develops director-driven projects across development, packaging and production.

The founders share a long-standing collaboration through the Finnish production collective Vegetarian Films (creative and credit history — not a legal affiliation).
